About

Space-Invaders-Clone is what it is; a clone of Space Invaders made for an intro to software engineering course. It was developed by three people when in actuality six people were supposed to work together on the assignment. Several modifications were made to date.

How to Play

Use the mouse to move left and right to dodge the Space Invaders assault! Use the left-click mouse button to shoot the Space Invaders down!
